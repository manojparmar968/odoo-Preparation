Return of investment(ROI) = (Net Profit/cost of investment)*100
                          = (Present value - cost of investment(COI)/ cost of investmentCOI)*100

Return on Advertising spend(ROAS) = Revenue from ADS/cost of ADS

working capital
    networking capital = current assets - current liabilities
    working capital ratio = current assets/current liabilities
Profit margin
    Net Profit margin = (net profit/net revenue)*100
    gross Profit margin = (revenue -COGS/revenue)*100

Earning before interest, tax,depreciation & amortization(EDITDA)
    EDITDA = operating income + depreciation + amortization

break even point(BEP) 
    Break Even Point (Units) = Fixed costs/selling price per unit OR variable cost per unit

Revenue Run Rate(RRR)
    RRR = Revenue for Period * Number of periods in year
